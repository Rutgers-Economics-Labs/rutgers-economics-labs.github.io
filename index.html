Our Mission

We are a student-led, data-driven research organization at Rutgers University that provides pro bono technical economic analysis for government agencies, think tanks, and public policy organizations. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic rigor and real-world policy impact.

Econometric Analysis

Advanced statistical modeling and causal inference using cutting-edge econometric techniques

Data Engineering

Large-scale data collection, cleaning, and pipeline development for policy research

Technical Research

Python, R, Stata, and SQL expertise for rigorous quantitative analysis

Policy Insights

Translating complex data into actionable recommendations for policymakers
